QuizMintAI Google Classroom Integration Backlog

Purpose
- integrate Google Classroom as a class-sync and assignment-distribution layer
- keep QuizMintAI as the primary workflow, analytics, and intervention system
- avoid duplicating full Google Classroom behavior inside QuizMintAI

Product Positioning
- QuizMintAI remains the source of truth for:
  - quiz generation
  - lesson-plan generation
  - assignment analytics
  - student results
  - intervention and reteach workflow
  - teacher workspace
- Google Classroom is used for:
  - class import
  - roster import
  - assignment publishing
  - teacher-facing distribution convenience

Integration Principles
- do not replace QuizMintAI classes with Google Classroom objects directly
- do not make Google Classroom the only source of truth
- store Google Classroom ids as linked external references on QuizMintAI records
- keep result review and intervention inside QuizMintAI
- design for partial sync, not full platform mirroring

What Should Stay Native In QuizMintAI
- generation
- saved libraries
- results review
- analytics
- intervention workflow
- teacher workspace

What Should Sync Or Publish To Google Classroom
- classes
- rosters
- assignment metadata
- due dates
- student-facing assignment links

What Should Not Be Rebuilt As A Full Duplicate
- full Google Classroom gradebook
- Classroom discussion stream
- all Classroom permission surfaces
- full submission experience inside Classroom

Completion Criteria
- teachers can connect Google Classroom from QuizMintAI
- teachers can import a class and roster into QuizMintAI
- teachers can publish a QuizMintAI assignment to Google Classroom
- QuizMintAI still remains the place where results and intervention happen
- sync and publish status are visible and recoverable

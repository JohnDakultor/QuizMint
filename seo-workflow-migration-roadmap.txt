SEO Workflow Migration Roadmap

Purpose
This roadmap defines how QuizMintAI should migrate its SEO positioning from a simple quiz-generation and lesson-plan-generation app into a full teacher workflow web app that uses LLMs to speed up planning, assignment creation, review, follow-up, and intervention work.

This is not a pricing roadmap.
This is not a generic marketing roadmap.
This is the SEO roadmap for changing what the product means in search.

Core SEO Thesis
- The product started with generator-style search intent:
  - ai quiz generator
  - free quiz generator
  - lesson plan generator
- The product has now grown into a broader teacher workflow product:
  - generate quizzes
  - generate lesson plans
  - assign work to classes
  - manage rosters
  - track results
  - follow up with reteach and intervention actions
- SEO should stop behaving like traffic acquisition for disconnected generators.
- SEO should become a workflow-intent acquisition system that brings in teachers, schools, tutors, and training teams who want to run class work faster with AI.

Desired Positioning In Search
- QuizMintAI is not just an AI quiz generator.
- QuizMintAI is a teacher workflow platform powered by LLMs.
- Generators are still entry points, but the SEO destination should become:
  - planning workflow
  - assignment workflow
  - quiz-results workflow
  - reteach and intervention workflow
  - teacher workspace workflow

Main SEO Problem
- Search architecture still carries old generator DNA.
- Public traffic can enter through generators, but the site still risks being interpreted as:
  - another AI content generator
  - another free quiz tool
  - a utility instead of a workflow platform
- The SEO migration is not finished until search engines, users, and landing-page journeys all reinforce:
  - this is a teacher workflow product
  - generators are part of the workflow, not the whole value

Migration Goal
- Preserve the demand already available around quiz and lesson-plan generation.
- Gradually shift authority toward higher-value workflow terms.
- Build content and internal linking so the strongest commercial intent sits around:
  - teacher workflow
  - classroom assignment workflow
  - quiz results review
  - reteach workflow
  - intervention workflow
  - teacher workspace

North Star Outcome
- When a teacher searches for quiz generation or lesson planning, QuizMintAI can rank.
- When a teacher searches for workflow problems after generation, QuizMintAI should rank even more strongly.
- Over time, higher-intent workflow pages should become more important than broad generator pages.
